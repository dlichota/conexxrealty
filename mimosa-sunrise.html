Description

Mimosa Sunrise villa on Tortola, British Virgin Islands is 5 minutes from Nanny Cay Marina and overlooks Sir Francis Drake Channel and the Caribbean Sea. There is a 180 deg. view of most of the sothern islands of the BVI including Norman Island, Peter Island and St john, USVI.


Mimosa Sunrise is airconditioned throughout and is well appointed with a fully equipped gourmet kitchen, lots of parking and easy access. The villa is on three levels and includes a kitchen on each level.


The top level has two ensuite bedrooms in two wings, a study, wet-bar, laundry room, and pantry. The large entrance foyer leads to the smaller of the two pools which connects to the wet-bar. The large pool has a swim-jet for those who like to swim withut doing laps. The west-wing bedroom opens to the pool deck and there is a guest bathroom next to the pool.


The east-wing bedroom opens to the large covered deck and privacy is ensured with a shutter separating it from the main deck.


The deck boasts a spectaclar 180 deg. view of the Sir Francis Drake Channel, from Virgin Gorda in the east to St John, USVI in the west. Most of the deck is covered but there is a large area next to the pool for the sun-worshippers.


The middle floor comprises a master bedroom suite in the east wing, a small kitchen, a full bathroom and an enormous family/entertainment area. There are also three more bedrooms and access to the pump room.


The bottom floor comprises two master suites, a small kitchen and a large living/dining area. There is also private parking and access.
