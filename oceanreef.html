Ocean Reef Waterfront

Description

Ocean Reef Waterfront is a two-storey house built in a modern style with covered decks, slidng-glass doors and tiled floors throughout. Comprising two bedrooms, two bathrooms, living room and kitchen.

Very easy access and no hills to navigate to a short gated drive with ample parking. The living, dining and kitchen are on the lower level and the bedrooms are accessed by an attractive flight of stairs.


The signature feature of Ocean Reef is its proximity to the Caribbean Sea with two covered decks from which to admire the stunning views over the ocean and some beautiful islands therein.


The centre of the house is the open kitchen, living room and breakfast bar, providing a natural environment in which to relax. The master bedroom has its own en-suite bathroom and opens onto the deck and magnificent views beyond.


The lower deck provides a spacious area for relaxing, barbequing and al fresco dining. From there it is only a few steps to the sea.
