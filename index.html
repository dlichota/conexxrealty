BVI APARTMENT

BVI rental apartment on Tortola, British Virgin Islands is 5 minutes from Nanny Cay Marina, 10 minutes from Road Town and 15 minutes to West End. Only a few minutes drive to the nearest beach and even the furthest beach is only 20 minutes drive. A wonderful view overlooking the Sir Francis Drake Channel and the Caribbean Sea, with a view of Norman Island and Peter Island and other islands of the BVI and USVI. BVI Apartment is an airconditioned, one bedroom, one bathroom apartment, well appointed with a fully equipped gourmet kitchen and free wifi. There is a sofa bed for a third person to sleep and a large wrap-around deck from which to enjoy your the view.
